Blood Pressure Measurement Guidelines

  • Ensure the patient is seated and relaxed for at least 5 minutes.
  • Back supported, feet flat, arm at heart level, no talking during measurement.
  • No caffeine/smoking/exercise 30 minutes before.
  • Use correct cuff size, wrap on bare arm.
  • Take two readings 1-2 minutes apart, record the average.
×

Blood Glucose Measurement Guidelines

  • Wash and dry hands before pricking.
  • Use a new lancet for each test.
  • Record whether fasting (8+ hours) or random (any time).
  • Apply drop of blood to strip, wait for result.
  • Clean meter regularly, calibrate if required.
